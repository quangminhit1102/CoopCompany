    Who We Are

    Southeast Asia’s leading Engineering Plastics, Machine Components and Electronic Assembly Tools provider. Founded in 1996, Pactumax worked closely with customers and built and developed 20+ years of expertise and experience, particularly in Engineering Plastics application

    Our Mission

    Our mission is to be a strong leader & preferred supplier in Asia for Engineering Plastics, Machine Components & Electronic Assembly Tools.
    Attaining & sustaining customer satisfaction is one of our mission.
